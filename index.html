Research Foundation

Built on Peer-Reviewed Science

Not built on vibes. Every model implements exact algorithms from published research — trained on real 3D mesh geometry.

NeurIPS 2017

PointNet++: Deep Hierarchical Feature Learning

Set abstraction on point clouds for part segmentation and 3D affordance prediction from real mesh vertices.

Models #6, #7 · 86.05% mIoU (paper benchmark)
ECCV 2022

Point-MAE: Masked Autoencoders for Point Clouds

Self-supervised pre-training on 3D geometry. 384-dim feature vectors capture structural complexity.

Model #8 · CD 0.0266 (paper benchmark)
CVPR 2023

ULIP: Unified 3D-Language-Image Pre-training

Tri-modal contrastive alignment. Maps 3D geometry to natural language — "arteries connecting heart to right lung."

Model #9 · 97.7% Zero-Shot (paper benchmark)
